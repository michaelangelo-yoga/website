Personal Training

One-on-one sessions designed around your body, your goals, and your schedule. Get focused guidance and faster progress with a plan built just for you.

Book a Session Explore Classes

Why Personal Training?

Private sessions offer the fastest, safest way to deepen your practice and reach your goals.

Tailored to You

Every session is built around your body, goals, injuries, and lifestyle. No generic routines—just targeted progress.

Flexible Scheduling

Train at a time that works for you. Sessions are available in-person at the studio or online from your home.

Expert Guidance

Receive real-time feedback on alignment, breath, and technique. Learn modifications that make every pose accessible and effective.

Accountability

Regular sessions keep you consistent. Together we track progress, adjust the plan, and celebrate milestones.

Holistic Approach

We combine movement, breathwork, mobility, and recovery strategies to support your overall wellbeing.

Safe & Supportive

Whether you are recovering from injury, managing a condition, or simply new to yoga, sessions are paced with care.

How It Works

1. Free consultation. We discuss your goals, health history, schedule, and any concerns.

2. Personalized plan. I design a program tailored to your body and objectives.

3. Train together. We meet in person or online for focused, progressive sessions.

4. Review & adjust. We regularly check in, refine the plan, and keep you moving forward.
